Executive Summary
The Rural Electrification Phase II project is currently 76% complete against a planned completion of 84% at this stage, reflecting a schedule slippage on critical-path activities tied to substation procurement. Financially, the project has disbursed $16.9M of its $24.6M budget (68.7%), broadly in line with physical progress. The Cost Performance Index (0.91) indicates mild cost overrun, while the Schedule Performance Index (0.79) signals a more significant schedule risk that the project team is addressing through an accelerated procurement track for the remaining minigrid lots.
